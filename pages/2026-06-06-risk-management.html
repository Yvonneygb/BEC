BEC Daily

风险管理 Risk Management

📅 2026年6月6日 · 星期六
A (Elena Rossi) — Chief Risk Officer, Meridian Bank
B (James Thornton) — Operations Director, Pacifica Shipping Ltd.

Dialogue

A:James, your quarterly risk register shows the South China Sea route contingency has been upgraded from amber to red. Walk me through the trigger event.
James，你们的季度风险登记册显示南海航线应急方案已从黄色升级为红色。带我了解一下触发事件。
B:Last Tuesday we received intelligence from our maritime security consultant that piracy incidents in the Malacca Strait are up 47% quarter-on-quarter. Three of our vessels pass through that corridor weekly. We had to escalate the risk rating.
上周二我们从海事安全顾问那里得到情报，马六甲海峡的海盗事件同比增长了47%。我们每周有三艘船经过那条走廊。我们不得不升级风险评级。
A:Understood. What mitigation measures have you activated so far? The board will want to see a proactive response, not just a flag on a dashboard.
理解。目前你们启动了哪些缓释措施？董事会希望看到主动应对，而不只是仪表盘上的一面旗。
B:We've rerouted two vessels to the Lombok Strait, which adds 2.3 days of sailing time but eliminates the exposure. For the remaining vessel — the Pacific Star — we've contracted an armed security detail at €18,000 per transit. The cost is significant, but it's within the risk appetite threshold we agreed on in Q1.
我们将两艘船改道龙目海峡，这增加了2.3天的航行时间，但消除了风险暴露。对于剩下那艘——太平洋之星号——我们以每次航行18000欧元的价格请了武装安保。成本不低，但在我们Q1约定的风险偏好阈值之内。
A:The Lombok reroute is sensible. On the armed security, I'd like to see a cost-benefit analysis against the alternative — what's the premium for war-risk insurance on that route versus the €18,000 per transit fee?
龙目海峡改道是合理的。关于武装安保，我想看看与替代方案的成本收益对比——那条航线的战争险保费与每次18000欧元的安保费相比如何？
B:Good question. Our broker quoted an additional war-risk premium of €142,000 annually for that specific corridor coverage, which amortises to roughly €2,730 per transit across 52 sailings. The insurance route is cheaper on paper, but — and this is the catch — the policy excludes loss of hire if the vessel is detained. A single six-day detention would cost us €420,000 in lost charter revenue.
好问题。我们的经纪人报价是全年额外战争险保费142000欧元，按52次航行摊销大约每次2730欧元。走保险在账面上更便宜，但——问题在这里——保单不覆盖船舶被扣押时的租金损失。一次六天的扣押将造成420000欧元的租金收入损失。
A:That tilts the equation decisively. Document this analysis in the risk register as the justification. Now, shifting gears — your supply chain concentration risk with that single-source semiconductor supplier in Hsinchu. The earthquake exposure needs quantification. What's the business continuity plan?
这使权衡结果明确倾斜了。把这个分析记录在风险登记册中作为依据。现在换个话题——你们在新竹那家单一来源半导体供应商的供应链集中风险。地震风险需要量化。业务连续性计划是什么？
B:We've been working on dual-sourcing. We qualified a secondary supplier in Penang last month, and we've pre-positioned eight weeks of buffer inventory at our Rotterdam hub. The total risk transfer cost — including the buffer stock carrying cost — is about €340,000. But it reduces our single-point-of-failure exposure by an estimated 82%.
我们一直在推进双重采购。上个月我们在槟城认证了一家二级供应商，并在鹿特丹枢纽预置了八周的缓冲库存。总风险转移成本——包括缓冲库存的持有成本——大约340000欧元。但这将我们的单点故障风险估计降低了82%。
A:Those metrics are exactly what the audit committee needs. One more item — have you stress-tested the cyber risk scenario since the Maersk-type ransomware incident hit the industry?
这些指标正是审计委员会需要的。还有一件事——自Maersk类型的勒索软件事件冲击行业以来，你们对网络风险进行了压力测试吗？
B:We ran a full penetration test last month. The good news: our OT systems are air-gapped. The bad news: our back-office ERP has three medium-severity vulnerabilities. Our CISO has a remediation timeline of six weeks. I've flagged it in the risk register as amber — controlled but requiring monitoring.
我们上个月做了一次全面渗透测试。好消息：我们的OT系统是物理隔离的。坏消息：我们后台的ERP系统有三个中等严重程度的漏洞。我们的CISO给出的修复时间表是六周。我已经在风险登记册中将其标为黄色——可控但需要监控。
A:Keep that timeline tight. I'll present the consolidated risk dashboard to the board on Thursday. Your shipping and supply chain risks are well-documented. Just ensure the cyber remediation doesn't slip past that six-week mark — regulators are showing zero tolerance on disclosures now.
严格把控这个时间表。周四我将向董事会呈报综合风险仪表盘。你们的航运和供应链风险记录得很好。只要确保网络修复不要超过六周这个期限——监管机构现在对信息披露持零容忍态度。
B:Understood. I'll have the CISO provide weekly progress updates copied to your office. We're also scheduling a tabletop exercise for a simulated ransomware attack in three weeks — you're welcome to observe.
明白。我会让CISO每周提供进展更新，抄送你的办公室。我们也计划在三周后进行一场模拟勒索软件攻击的桌面演练——欢迎你来观摩。
A:I'll be there. That's exactly the kind of proactive governance we need to demonstrate.
我一定来。这正是我们需要展现的主动治理。

Key Vocabulary

risk register — 风险登记册 | 系统记录和追踪各类企业风险的文件，BEC高频词
mitigation measures — 缓释措施 | 降低风险影响或发生概率的行动方案
risk appetite — 风险偏好 | 组织愿意承担的风险水平，董事会治理核心术语
single-point-of-failure — 单点故障 | 供应链和IT领域的关键风险概念
dual-sourcing — 双重采购 | 通过两个供应商分散供应链风险的策略
buffer inventory — 缓冲库存 | 为应对供应中断而储备的额外库存
penetration test — 渗透测试 | 模拟攻击以评估系统安全性的测试
air-gapped — 物理隔离的 | 未连接外部网络的系统，最高安全级别措施
tabletop exercise — 桌面演练 | 模拟危机情景的讨论式演练

Sentence Patterns

Walk me through the trigger event. → 带我把触发事件过一遍 | 要求对方详细解释某事起因
We had to escalate the risk rating. → 我们不得不升级风险评级 | 描述风险等级上升
What's the business continuity plan? → 业务连续性计划是什么？ | 询问危机应对方案的标准问法
That tilts the equation decisively. → 这使权衡结果明确倾斜 | 用于表达某一因素彻底改变了决策方向
The good news... The bad news... → 好消息是……坏消息是…… | 用于平衡地呈现风险评估结果
Regulators are showing zero tolerance on... → 监管机构对……持零容忍态度 | 强调合规要求紧迫性

Learning Tip

风险管理是BEC考试中口语Part 2（mini-presentation）和写作Part 2（report）的高频话题。建议重点掌握风险等级描述（amber/red/escalate）和缓释措施相关词汇。练习小演讲时，可以用"识别风险→量化影响→描述措施→评估效果"的框架。桌面演练（tabletop exercise）和渗透测试（penetration test）是近年BEC阅读新增的术语，需要特别关注。
