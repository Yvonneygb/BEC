邮件写作 Email Writing

📅 2026年6月6日 · 星期六
A (Linda Park) — Senior Buyer, Pacific Retail Group
B (Tom Hart) — Account Manager, Swift Logistics Ltd.

Dialogue

A:Tom, I've just read your email thread and there are a few things I want to clarify. Your last message mentioned the shipment was delayed, but you didn't include the revised dispatch date or the tracking reference. That's left our warehouse team in the dark.
Tom，我刚看了你的邮件线程，有几件事想确认。你上一封邮件提到货物延迟了，但没有包含修改后的发货日期或追踪号。这让我们仓库团队一头雾水。
B:You're right, Linda — my apologies. Let me pull up the details now. The shipment was held at customs for a random inspection, which pushed everything back by three working days. I sent a follow-up with the revised timeline to your procurement team, but it looks like you weren't cc'd on that.
你是对的，Linda——抱歉。我现在调出详情。货物在海关被抽检了，整个流程推迟了三个工作日。我给你们的采购团队发了后续更新，但看起来没有抄送你。
A:That explains the gap. In future, please make sure I'm always on the distribution list. Also, your quotation for the Q4 bulk order arrived, but the unit pricing on line items 7 through 12 doesn't match what we agreed in the term sheet. There's a discrepancy of roughly 8%.
这就说得通了。以后请确保我一定在分发名单上。另外，Q4批量订单的报价单收到了，但第7到12行的单价与我们条款清单中约定的不一致，有大约8%的差异。
B:That's concerning. Let me look into that straight away. Could you forward me the email with the original agreed terms so I can cross-reference? I suspect the pricing table wasn't updated after our last negotiation round.
这需要关注。我马上去查。能否把原始约定条款的邮件转发给我，我好做交叉比对？我怀疑是上次谈判后价格表没更新。
A:Done — just forwarded it. The subject line reads "Swift Logistics — Q4 Bulk Order Confirmed v3". While we're at it, I need your team to acknowledge receipt of the return batch we shipped last Monday. We haven't had any confirmation, and our finance team is waiting to reconcile the credit note.
转发了——主题是"Swift Logistics — Q4 Bulk Order Confirmed v3"。顺带一提，我需要你们团队确认收到上周一发出的退货批次。我们至今没收到任何确认，财务团队在等着核销贷项通知单。
B:Noted. I'll chase that up with our returns department. For the Q4 order, once I've ironed out the pricing issue, I'll send you a revised pro-forma invoice. I'd appreciate it if you could review at your earliest convenience so we don't miss the production slot.
收到。我去催退货部门。至于Q4订单，一旦我解决了定价问题，就给你发修订后的形式发票。如能在方便时尽早审阅，以免错过生产窗口。
A:Will do. One more thing — in your last email, you used the phrase "ASAP" three times. I know it's minor, but for formal correspondence with our director, could you be a bit more precise with timelines? "By close of business Thursday" is much clearer.
好的。还有一件事——你上一封邮件里用了三次"ASAP"。我知道这是小事，但在给总监的正式通信中，能否把时间线说得更具体一些？"周四下班前"就清楚得多。
B:Point taken, Linda. I'll be more specific going forward. I'll also include a clear call to action in each email so there's no ambiguity about what's needed from your end.
说得对，Linda。以后我会更具体。每封邮件我都会加上明确的行动号召，这样你这边需要做什么就不会模糊不清了。
A:That would help enormously. Looking forward to the revised invoice. And Tom — on the whole, your team's responsiveness has been excellent. This is housekeeping, not a complaint.
这将大有帮助。期待修订后的发票。另外 Tom——总体而言，你们团队的响应速度一直很好。这只是完善流程，不是投诉。
B:Appreciate that, Linda. I'll have the amended quotation with the call to action and revised timeline in your inbox within two hours. If anything's still off, just shoot me an email and I'll jump on it.
感谢，Linda。两小时内我会把修正后的报价单发到你邮箱，附上行动号召和修订后的时间线。如果还有问题，直接发邮件给我，我马上处理。
A:Perfect. Two hours works. Let's aim to have everything signed off by Friday. Thanks, Tom.
完美。两小时没问题。目标周五前全部签批完毕。谢谢，Tom。

Key Vocabulary

dispatch — 发货/配送 | 物流和供应链邮件中的核心词汇，BEC 高频考点
cc'd — 抄送 | 邮件场景核心术语，动词形式"to cc someone"
quotation — 报价单 | 与 estimate（估算）不同，quotation 是具有约束力的正式报价
discrepancy — 差异/出入 | 商务信函中专业地指出数据不一致的说法
acknowledge receipt — 确认收到 | 正式邮件中用于确认收悉的标准短语
reconcile — 对账/核对 | 财务会计场景核心词汇，指核对两套记录的一致性
iron out — 解决/消除 | 商务口语中表示解决小问题或分歧
at your earliest convenience — 在你方便时尽早 | 正式邮件中的礼貌用语，注意语气比"as soon as possible"更委婉
call to action — 行动号召/明确要求 | 邮件结尾明确指出期望对方做的具体行动
shoot me an email — 给我发封邮件（口语） | 非正式商务沟通中的常用表达

Sentence Patterns

I want to clarify... → 我想确认/澄清一下…… | 商务邮件开头引出疑问的常用句型
Please make sure I'm... → 请确保我被…… | 礼貌但坚定地要求被纳入沟通圈
Could you forward me... so I can...? → 能否转发……以便我……？ | 邮申请求对方提供信息的标准句型
I'd appreciate it if you could... → 如能……我将不胜感激 | 正式邮件中的高频礼貌请求句式
Looking forward to... → 期待…… | 邮件结尾的经典表述，注意后面接名词或动名词

Learning Tip

BEC中级写作Part 2常考商务邮件/信函。重点掌握三个层次的语言切换：正式请求（I would appreciate it if you could...）、中性确认（Could you clarify...）、非正式跟进（Just shoot me an email）。邮件写作的核心是"清晰 > 华丽"——每封邮件必须包含明确的主题行（subject line）、单一清晰的行动号召（call to action）和具体的截止时间。想象你是收件人，收到这封邮件后能否在30秒内知道要做什么，是检验邮件质量的标准。
