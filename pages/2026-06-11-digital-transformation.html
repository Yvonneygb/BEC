BEC Daily

数字化转型 Digital Transformation

📅 2026年6月11日 · 星期四
A (Linda Zhao) — Chief Digital Officer, EastBridge Manufacturing
B (David Kim) — Senior Consultant, TechVantage Solutions

Dialogue

A:David, thank you for coming in. As you know, EastBridge has been a traditional manufacturer for over 30 years. We're now looking to embark on a comprehensive digital transformation, but I'll be honest — we're not entirely sure where to start.
David，感谢你过来。如你所知，EastBridge作为传统制造企业已运营超过30年。我们现在希望开启全面的数字化转型，但说实话——我们不太确定从哪里着手。
B:That's completely understandable, Linda. Many mid-sized manufacturers are in the same boat. The first thing I'd recommend is conducting a thorough audit of your current legacy systems. Without understanding what you're working with, any transformation effort is shooting in the dark.
这完全可以理解，Linda。很多中型制造商都面临同样处境。我首先建议对你们当前的遗留系统进行一次彻底的审计。不了解手头有什么，任何转型努力都是盲人摸象。
A:We've already identified that our ERP system from 2008 is one of the biggest bottlenecks. Order processing still involves manual data entry in three different departments. Our IT director estimates it costs us roughly 400 hours a month in redundant labour.
我们已经发现，2008年上线的ERP系统是最大的瓶颈之一。订单处理仍然涉及三个不同部门的人工数据录入。我们IT总监估计每月在重复性劳动上花费大约400小时。
B:That's substantial. And that's exactly the kind of low-hanging fruit we'd target in a pilot program. I'd suggest starting with automating the order-to-cash cycle — you'd see measurable ROI within the first two quarters, which helps build momentum for the broader initiative.
这个数字相当可观。而这正是我们在试点项目中要瞄准的"低垂果实"。我建议从订单到现金的自动化流程入手——前两个季度就能看到可衡量的投资回报率，这有助于为更广泛的转型积累动力。
A:I like the sound of that. However, the biggest hurdle I anticipate isn't technical — it's cultural. Our shop floor supervisors have been doing things the same way for 15 years. Change management is going to be critical.
这听起来不错。但我预判最大的障碍不是技术上的——而是文化层面的。我们车间的班组长15年来一直用同样的方式做事。变革管理将至关重要。
B:You've hit on the single most important factor. I've seen more digital transformations fail due to poor change management than due to technology issues. I'd recommend we establish a network of digital champions across your plants — people on the ground who can demonstrate the tangible benefits.
你抓住了最关键的因素。我见过更多数字化转型失败是由于变革管理不善，而非技术问题。我建议在各工厂建立一支数字化先锋网络——让一线人员展示实实在在的好处。
A:That's a clever approach. My concern, though, is scalability. If we roll this out across all seven plants simultaneously, we risk overwhelming our workforce. What's your view on a phased rollout?
这个方法很聪明。不过我担心的是可扩展性。如果我们同时在所有七个工厂推广，可能会让员工不堪重负。你对分阶段部署怎么看？
B:A phased rollout is absolutely the right call. I'd suggest we pick one plant as the pilot, run it for 12 weeks, then use the data from that to make the business case to the other plant managers. The numbers speak for themselves when you can show a 20% reduction in processing time.
分阶段部署绝对是正确的选择。我建议选一个工厂作为试点，运行12周，然后用数据向其他工厂经理展示商业案例。当你能够展示处理时间缩短20%，数据本身就能说明问题。
A:I'm with you on that. But we also need to get buy-in from key stakeholders at the board level. Our CFO is notoriously conservative when it comes to IT spending. How do we frame this so it doesn't just look like another expensive tech project?
我同意。但我们也需要获得董事会层面关键利益相关者的支持。我们的CFO在IT支出方面出了名的保守。我们怎么包装这个项目，让它看起来不像又一个昂贵的IT工程？
B:Frame it as a revenue enabler, not a cost item. Cloud migration, for instance, isn't just about IT infrastructure — it's about giving your sales team real-time access to inventory data on mobile devices, which directly translates to faster quote turnaround and higher win rates.
把它定位为收入增长引擎，而不是成本项目。比如云迁移，它不仅仅是IT基础设施的问题——它能让你的销售团队在移动设备上实时获取库存数据，直接转化为更快的报价周转和更高的中标率。
A:That's exactly the framing we need. One more thing — digital literacy across our workforce is uneven at best. Some of our senior technicians can barely use email. Do you offer training programmes as part of the implementation package?
这正是我们需要的定位。还有一件事——我们员工的数字素养参差不齐。有些资深技师连邮件都不太会用。你们在实施服务中提供培训项目吗？
B:We do — and it's baked into every phase. We provide role-specific training modules, from basic digital literacy for the shop floor to advanced analytics for your management team. It's not an add-on; it's integral to the methodology.
我们有——而且培训已经嵌入每个阶段。我们提供按岗位定制的培训模块，从面向车间的基础数字素养，到面向管理团队的高级数据分析。这不是附加项，而是方法论的核心组成部分。

Key Vocabulary

digital transformation — 数字化转型 | 企业运用数字技术重塑业务流程、文化和客户体验的战略过程
legacy system — 遗留系统/老旧系统 | 仍在运行但技术过时的旧IT系统，BEC阅读中常出现在企业升级改造场景
ROI (Return on Investment) — 投资回报率 | 衡量投资效益的核心财务指标，商务提案的必备概念
scalability — 可扩展性 | 系统或方案能否随着业务增长而灵活扩展的能力
phased rollout — 分阶段部署 | 将大型项目拆分为阶段逐步推进的实施策略，降低风险
change management — 变革管理 | 引导组织和个人从当前状态过渡到目标状态的结构化方法
key stakeholder — 关键利益相关者 | 对项目成败有重大影响的个人或群体，需优先获得其支持（buy-in）
pilot program — 试点项目 | 在全面推广前先在小范围测试的验证性项目
cloud migration — 云迁移 | 将数据、应用从本地服务器迁移到云计算平台的过程
digital literacy — 数字素养 | 员工使用数字工具和技术的基本能力，数字化转型的软性基础

Sentence Patterns

The first thing I'd recommend is... → 我首先建议的是…… | 咨询顾问向客户提出建议的开场句式
The biggest hurdle I anticipate isn't... — it's... → 我预判最大障碍不是……而是…… | 对比式表达，强调真正的难点
I'd suggest we... → 我建议我们…… | 提出行动方案的委婉表达方式
The numbers speak for themselves. → 数据本身就能说明问题 | 用事实/数据作为论据的有力表达
Frame it as..., not... → 将其定位为……，而不是…… | 重新包装/定义问题的话术，适用于说服场景
It's not an add-on; it's integral to... → 这不是附加项，而是……的核心组成部分 | 强调某事物不可或缺的地位
