电话沟通 Telephone Communication

📅 2026年6月6日 · 星期六
A (Jack Morrison) — Supply Chain Coordinator, Atlas Manufacturing
B (Sophie Keller) — Customer Service Rep, EuroFast Shipping

Dialogue

A:Good morning, this is Jack Morrison calling from Atlas Manufacturing. Could I speak to someone regarding order EF-8842, please? The automated tracking hasn't updated in four days and I'm getting rather anxious.
早上好，我是Atlas Manufacturing的Jack Morrison。我想就订单EF-8842咨询相关人员。自动追踪系统已四天未更新，我相当焦虑。
B:Good morning, Mr Morrison. This is Sophie Keller speaking. Let me pull up your order on the system. Could you give me your account number or the order reference while I search?
早上好，Morrison先生。我是Sophie Keller。让我在系统中调出您的订单。能否在我搜索时提供您的账号或订单编号？
A:Certainly — the order reference is EF-8842, and our account number is ATL-77630. I was told the consignment would arrive by Tuesday, but it's now Thursday and our production line is about to grind to a halt.
当然——订单编号EF-8842，账号ATL-77630。之前被告知货物周二前到，但现在已是周四，我们的生产线即将停顿。
B:I can see the order here, Mr Morrison. It appears the consignment was held up at the Rotterdam hub due to a backlog caused by the bank holiday weekend. I do apologise for the lack of communication — that shouldn't have happened. Let me find out where your pallets are right now. Would you mind holding for a moment while I contact the depot?
我在系统中看到了这个订单，Morrison先生。货物似乎在鹿特丹中转站因银行假日周末造成的积压而耽搁了。对于沟通缺失我很抱歉——这种情况本不该发生。让我查清您的货盘现在的位置。您介意稍等片刻，让我联系中转站吗？
A:I'd rather not — we've been on the line for a while. Could you call me back on the number I'm calling from? I have a site meeting in fifteen minutes. I'll be reachable again after eleven.
最好还是不要——我们已经通话一段时间了。能否用我当前拨打的号码给我回电？十五分钟后我有个现场会议，十一点后可以再接电话。
B:Absolutely. I've noted your contact number. I'll get to the bottom of this and ring you back by 11:15 sharp with a concrete update. May I also suggest I set up SMS alerts on your order so you receive real-time updates going forward? That way you won't have to rely on the automated tracking alone.
完全没问题。我已记下您的联系号码。我会彻底查明此事，在十一点十五分整回电给您具体的最新进展。另外，我能否建议为您的订单开通短信提醒，以便往后实时接收更新？这样您就不用只依赖自动追踪系统了。
A:Yes, please do — that would be helpful. While you're at it, can you confirm whether the remaining two pallets have even left the Rotterdam hub? If they haven't, I need to explore contingency options with a local supplier to tide us over.
好的，请开通——这会很有帮助。另外，能否确认剩下两托货是否已离开鹿特丹中转站？如果还没有，我需要与本地供应商探讨应急方案来维持生产。
B:That's a very sensible question. Let me check — I can see that pallet one of three and two of three were scanned out of Rotterdam at 08:12 this morning. It's pallet three that's still showing as pending. They may have been split onto different vehicles. Give me until 11:15 to confirm and I'll present you with a clear picture of the delivery schedule.
这个问题问得很到位。让我查看——我看到三托中的第一托和第二托今早08:12已扫描离港鹿特丹。第三托仍显示待处理状态。它们可能被分到了不同车辆上。给我到十一点十五分的时间确认，我会为您呈现清晰的派送时间表。
A:Three pallets split across different trucks? That's exactly the kind of detail the tracking system should communicate but doesn't. I appreciate you being upfront about it, Sophie. I'll wait for your call at eleven.
三托货分到不同卡车上？这正是追踪系统应该告知却不告知的细节。感谢你的坦诚相告，Sophie。我等十一点的来电。
B:I completely understand your frustration, Mr Morrison. I'll personally escalate the tracking visibility issue to our IT team as well — it's a recurring complaint we need to address. One more thing: would you like me to notify your warehouse contact directly once the delivery window is confirmed?
我完全理解您的沮丧，Morrison先生。我也会亲自将这个追踪可见性问题升级给我们的IT团队——这是需要解决的反复投诉。还有一件事：派送时间确认后，是否需要我直接通知贵方仓库联系人？
A:That's a great idea. The warehouse supervisor is Martin Reeves — his direct line is in the account notes. Loop him in as well. I'll make sure he's expecting your call. Thanks for taking ownership of this, Sophie. It's reassuring.
好主意。仓库主管是Martin Reeves——他的直线电话在账户备注里。也请他加入联络。我会确保他知道你的来电。感谢你主动承担这件事，Sophie。让人安心。
B:Not at all, it's what I'm here for. So, to recap: I'll trace the three pallets, ring you back at 11:15 with a confirmed delivery timeline, set up SMS alerts, notify Martin Reeves at your warehouse, and escalate the tracking issue to IT. Anything else while I have you?
不用客气，这是我应该做的。那么，概括一下：我将追踪三托货物，十一点十五分回复确认派送时间，开通短信提醒，通知贵仓库的Martin Reeves，并将追踪问题升级给IT。还有什么其他需要吗？
A:That covers it. I'll speak to you soon. Goodbye for now, Sophie.
就这些。稍后聊。先再见了，Sophie。
B:Goodbye, Mr Morrison. You'll hear from me before 11:15.
再见，Morrison先生。十一点十五分前您会收到我的答复。

Key Vocabulary

pull up your order — 调出您的订单 | 客服电话中表示在系统中查找订单信息的常用表达
held up — 被耽搁/延误 | 比"delayed"更口语化，电话沟通中常用
backlog — 积压 | 物流和生产场景中表示工作堆积导致延误
depot — 仓库/中转站 | 物流行业核心词汇，指货物中转或存放的节点
call me back — 给我回电 | 电话沟通基础词汇，但考试中常搭配具体时间出现
get to the bottom of this — 彻底查明此事 | 客服场景中表示会追查到底的承诺用语
contingency — 应急方案/备用计划 | 商务场景中表示应对突发情况的替代方案
pending — 待处理/悬而未决 | 系统状态描述高频词，BEC 阅读常考
upfront — 坦率的/开诚布公的 | 口语中表示诚实、不隐瞒的态度
escalate — 升级处理 | 表示将问题上报给更高级别或专业部门处理

Sentence Patterns

Could I speak to someone regarding...? → 我可以找人就……事谈谈吗？ | 电话开场白，比"Can I talk to..."更正式
Would you mind holding for a moment? → 您介意稍等一会儿吗？ | 电话中请对方等待的礼貌用语
I'd rather not — could you...? → 我不想……能否……？ | 礼貌地拒绝并给出替代方案，比直接说"no"更专业
May I also suggest...? → 我能否也建议……？ | 客服提出增值建议的委婉说法
To recap: ... → 概括一下：…… | 电话结束时总结确认所有要点

Learning Tip

电话沟通是BEC中级听力的高频场景（尤其Part 1的电话留言和Part 3的客户服务对话）。关键训练点：数字和编号的快速辨识能力（订单号、账号、电话号码），这是中国考生的常见失分点。建议练习时先用1.0倍速听懂大意，再用1.2倍速精听捕捉具体数据。同时注意电话中的"澄清请求"句型（Would you mind repeating... / I didn't quite catch...），考试中常以这些句子为信号提示后面是关键信息。
