BEC Daily

危机公关 Crisis Management

📅 2026年6月6日 · 星期六
A (Claire Morgan) — VP of Communications, NutriLife Foods
B (Daniel Kwon) — CEO, NutriLife Foods

Dialogue

A:Daniel, we have a situation. A food safety watchdog in Belgium just published a report claiming traces of an undeclared allergen were found in our oat bars. It's trending on social media — #NutriLifeRecall is already at twelve thousand posts. We need to activate the crisis protocol immediately.
Daniel，我们有情况了。比利时一家食品安全监督机构刚刚发布报告，称在我们的燕麦棒中检出了未申报的过敏原痕迹。社交媒体上已经在疯传——#NutriLifeRecall已经有一万两千条帖子了。我们需要立即启动危机应对预案。
B:Good Lord. How credible is the source? Have we verified the claim internally before we issue any statement? I don't want us to overreact and validate something that might be an isolated lab error.
天哪。消息来源有多可信？在对外发布任何声明之前，我们内部有没有验证这个说法？我可不想反应过度反而坐实了一个可能只是实验室孤例错误的东西。
A:Our QA team is running batch tests as we speak. Preliminary results should be back within the hour. In the meantime, we've got three retailers — one in Brussels, two in Amsterdam — that have pulled the product from shelves proactively. We either get ahead of this or let it define us.
我们的品控团队正在做批次测试。初步结果一小时内应该能出来。与此同时，已经有三家零售商——布鲁塞尔一家、阿姆斯特丹两家——主动将产品从货架上撤下。我们要么抢在前面掌控局面，要么让它定义我们。
B:Agreed. What's the immediate course of action you're recommending? I want to be transparent but not alarmist — there's a fine line between responsible disclosure and feeding the panic.
同意。你建议的即时行动方案是什么？我想做到透明但不要危言耸听——负责任的信息披露和助长恐慌之间只有一线之隔。
A:First, we issue a holding statement within the next thirty minutes. Something like: "NutriLife is aware of the report and is investigating as a matter of urgency. Consumer safety is our top priority." Short, factual, no speculation. Second, we set up a dedicated press hotline and a FAQ page on our website. Third, if our tests confirm the finding, we initiate a voluntary recall across the Benelux region.
首先，在三十分钟内发布一份暂定声明。内容类似："NutriLife已注意到该报告，正作为紧急事项开展调查。消费者安全是我们的首要任务。"简短、事实性、不做猜测。第二，设立专门的媒体热线和官网FAQ页面。第三，如果我们的检测确认了该发现，我们就在比荷卢地区发起主动召回。
B:What's the financial exposure if we do a full recall? And how fast can we trace the affected batches?
如果全面召回，财务敞口有多大？另外，我们多快能追溯到受影响批次？
A:Our traceability system can pinpoint the batches within two hours — we invested in that after the 2019 dairy incident. The recall would affect roughly 180,000 units across Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. Estimated cost including logistics, retailer compensation, and disposal is around €2.1 million. Plus whatever the brand damage translates to.
我们的追溯系统能在两小时内精确定位批次——我们是在2019年乳制品事件后投资建成的。召回将影响比利时、荷兰和卢森堡约18万件产品。估计成本包括物流、零售商补偿和销毁费用，大约210万欧元。再加上品牌受损带来的影响。
B:Right. And if the tests come back negative? We've still taken a reputational hit. What's our messaging strategy to rebuild trust?
明白。如果检测结果是阴性呢？我们已经承受了声誉受损。重建信任的信息策略是什么？
A:If the tests are clear, we pivot to aggressive transparency — we publish the full lab report, invite third-party auditors to verify, and offer a brief in-store promotional campaign to win back shelf space. I've already drafted both messaging tracks so we can deploy instantly once we have the lab data. No lag time.
如果检测结果没问题，我们转向主动透明策略——公布完整实验室报告、邀请第三方审计核实、开展一次简短的店内促销活动来夺回货架位置。我两条信息轨道都已经起草好了，一旦拿到实验数据就能即时部署，零滞后期。
B:Good. I'll brief the board and coordinate with legal on liability exposure. You handle the comms and the regulatory liaison with the Belgian food safety authority. We'll reconvene in ninety minutes when the lab results are in. And Claire — I want us to be the gold standard in how to handle this. If we mess this up, the share price won't be the only casualty.
好的。我去通报董事会并和法务协调责任风险敞口。你来负责对外沟通和与比利时食品安全局的监管联络。待会九十分钟后等实验数据出来再碰。Claire——我希望我们成为处理此类事件的黄金标杆。如果搞砸了，受害的不只是股价。
A:Understood. I'll notify the social media team to pause all scheduled posts and route all incoming queries to the crisis desk. One question — should we pre-emptively notify our major retail partners in the UK and Germany, even though the report only mentions the Benelux?
明白。我通知社交媒体团队暂停所有排期发布、将全部问询转至危机处理台。有一个问题——报告只提到比荷卢，我们应该先发制人地通知英国和德国的主要零售合作伙伴吗？
B:Yes. Better to over-communicate internally and with trade partners than have them find out through the media. Send a brief heads-up email to the top ten accounts. Emphasise it's precautionary. I'd rather they hear it from us first.
要。宁可对内部和贸易伙伴过度沟通，也不要让他们通过媒体得知。给前十的大客户发一封简短的提前告知邮件。强调这是预防性的。我宁愿他们先从我们这里听到。

Key Vocabulary

crisis protocol — 危机应对预案 | 企业为紧急负面事件预设的标准化处理流程
holding statement — 暂定声明/临时声明 | 危机初期发布的简短立场声明，用于争取时间
voluntary recall — 主动召回 | 企业在政府强制之前自主发起的产品召回行动
traceability — 可追溯性 | 通过供应链追踪产品来源和流向的能力
reputational hit — 声誉受损 | 负面事件对公司品牌形象的冲击
liability exposure — 责任风险敞口 | 公司在法律和财务层面面临的潜在赔偿风险
regulatory liaison — 监管联络 | 与政府监管机构之间的正式沟通和协调
precautionary — 预防性的 | 为避免风险而采取的提前措施，危机沟通中的关键词

Sentence Patterns

We either get ahead of this or let it define us. → 我们要么抢在前面掌控局面，要么让它定义我们 | 危机管理中鼓舞行动的强力表达
There's a fine line between responsible disclosure and feeding the panic. → 负责任的信息披露和助长恐慌之间只有一线之隔 | 危机沟通中分寸感的经典表达
What's the financial exposure if...? → 如果……我们的财务敞口有多大？ | 量化风险影响的标准提问
I want us to be the gold standard in how to handle this. → 我希望我们成为处理此类事件的黄金标杆 | gold standard = 最高标准/典范
Better to over-communicate internally than have them find out through the media. → 宁可内部过度沟通，也不要让他们通过媒体得知 | 危机管理的核心原则
I'd rather they hear it from us first. → 我宁愿他们先从我们这里听到 | 强调主动沟通的重要性

Learning Tip

危机公关（Crisis Management）是BEC中级阅读Part 5（句子填空）和口语Part 3（协作讨论）的热门话题。备考时建议掌握危机管理的基本时间线：检测→评估→回应→修复（detect → assess → respond → recover）。口语考试中，如果遇到危机场景的讨论题，建议先明确立场（prioritise consumer safety），然后按"即时措施+中期方案+长期修复"三段式展开。关键词voluntary recall、holding statement、damage control要能脱口而出，这在考试紧张状态下尤为关键。
